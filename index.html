FAQ

Aksar Puche Jaane Wale Sawaal

Compost powder kya hota hai aur kahan milta hai?+
Compost powder ek microbial mix hota hai jo decomposition ko speed up karta hai. Amazon aur local garden shops mein milta hai. Main link Finds section mein share karti hoon!
Compost mein smell kyun aata hai?+
Zyada moisture ya oxygen ki kami se smell aata hai. Compost powder dale — smell bilkul khatam ho jaata hai!
Compost ready hua hai kaise pata chalega?+
Jab dark brown/black ho, crumbly texture ho aur soil jaise smell aaye — ready hai! Koi original waste visible nahi hona chahiye.
Kitna time lagta hai compost banane mein?+
Normal compost 45-60 din mein ready hota hai. Compost powder use karein toh 30-40 din mein bhi ho sakta hai!

Meri Kahani 🌱

Sab shuru hua ek chhote se ghar se jahan ek balcony thi. Ek din socha — kyun na yahan kuch ugaya jaaye? Pehle chhoti si seedling tray, phir ek pot, phir doosra... aur aaj mera poora balcony ek mini jungle ban chuka hai!

Compost banana tab seekha jab market ki sabzi se tang aa gayi. Kitchen waste ko zameen mein milaya, compost powder dala, 45 din baad jo dark brown crumbly material nikla — woh mera sabse pehla homemade compost tha. Us din ki khushi alag hi thi!

Aur Meesho aur Amazon ki finds? Woh isliye share karta hoon kyunki main khud bahut paisa galat jagah waste kar chuki hoon. Ab aap mat karo woh galti! 😄
