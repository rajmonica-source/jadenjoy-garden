PUT YOUR GARDEN PHOTOS HERE
============================
Rename your photos exactly like this:

garden1.jpg   → Your main garden wide photo
garden2.jpg   → Another garden angle
tomatoes.jpg  → Your tomato plants / harvest
sabzi.jpg     → Fresh vegetables photo
seedlings.jpg → New plant seedlings

TIPS:
- Use .jpg or .png format
- Resize photos to max 1200px wide before uploading (use free site: squoosh.app)
- Keep file size under 500KB for fast loading
